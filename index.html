Bookmarklet: Activate iPython Notebook lecture mode

To use:

  1. Add the bookmarklet to your browser.
  2. Open up an IPython notebook.
  3. Run the bookmarklet. The lecture controls should appear in the bottom left corner of the screen.
  4. Click 'Pick audio URL' and put in a URL for an audio file. Here are some example files:
    • http://wolever.github.io/browsercast/examples/example.mp3
    • http://wolever.github.io/browsercast/examples/example.ogg
  5. Use ctrl-l h to see a list of available keyboard shortcuts.
  6. Click 'Playback' in the bottom left to enter playback mode. Note: you may need to rewind the audio to the beginning first!
